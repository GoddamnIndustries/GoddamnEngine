In this directory are located specifications header from OpenGL(ES). This files are not directly used by the engine, otherwise they are given to special parser utility that can generate loaded correctly API.
All sources where downloaded directly from http://www.khronos.org/registry/gles/

*** Q&A ***

Q: Why ES? 
A: For compatibility.

Q: Why headers have strange ".Non-Source-C-Header" extension?
A: To make code-lines-counter not consider this file.
